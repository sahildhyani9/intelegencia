Glossary
Impressions: The total number of times your content (post, article, update, or ad) is displayed on someone's screen. This includes multiple views by the same user. It measures the reach of your content.

Reactions: The total number of interactions (likes, comments, shares, and other reactions) your content receives. It indicates how engaging your content is to your audience.

Clicks: The total number of times users click on your content, company page, or ad. This includes clicks on links, hashtags, your profile, or any other clickable element. It measures user interest and engagement.

Page Views: The total number of times your LinkedIn page (company or personal) is viewed. This includes repeated views by the same user. It helps gauge the overall visibility of your page.

Unique Visitors: The number of individual users who visit your LinkedIn page or content within a specific time frame. Unlike page views, this metric counts each user only once, regardless of how many times they visit. It provides insight into the size of your audience.
